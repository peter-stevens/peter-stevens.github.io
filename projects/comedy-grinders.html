COMEDY

grinders

A web series that I co-write, direct, and edit in Montreal. In the words of Alain, "All who struggle to make art and ends meet pass through the grind."

Alain wanted to explore the local community of artists who follow their passions, grinding out show after show, refusing to be discouraged.

I wanted to edit a comedy in the rapid fire style of Jake and Amir and Thirty Rock.

Our formula for making Grinders is to start with a theme that interests us, bury it in silly jokes, and include a passionate, poignant rant.

I spent a month and a half in Montreal as Theatre Ste-Catherine's artist in residence. I had a rough ending to 2011 and living in that space, with its community of comedians, writers, actors, and improvisers, saved me. It righted my ship.

I really enjoyed the process. It takes two hours to shoot an episode and I would often be able to have the final edit the next day.
