COMEDY

The Irrelevant Show

I write comedy sketches for a show on CBC radio. It's very exciting to work for The Irrelevant Show. I really like having surreal Skype conversations with producer Peter Brown.

PETER (me)
How about a girl who isn't doing well in school because it turns out she's a falcon?

PETER (him)
Sounds good. Maybe as a parent-teacher interview?

PETER (me)
Perfect.

PETER (him)
Green light for that one.

I have a great time brainstorming with Peter and then I sit in a coffee shop and write. I email a draft, respond to his insightful notes, and then I get to hear his talented cast breathe life into it.

You can tune in on CBC Radio, Saturdays at 11am.

Here's some of my best work on the show so far.

Bee Circus
The owner of a bee circus holds a press release about the disaster.

Nonsensed
A man without sight, hearing, smell, taste, and touch lives by making good guesses.

Orange Juice
A wife's curious injuries from a car accident leave her looking identical to her husband.
