Elephant Empire

This is my award-winning sketch comedy troupe.

We have new live show every Wednesday in Toronto. We're headed in an exciting new direction. We perform a short, three act play within a show filled with sketch comedy.

We started with videos like Spacefighters, Newscaster, and this commercial for "Shampo".

Next came this 22-minute pilot.

This was the first shot at my ideal comedy show: a short film in three acts with silliness in between. The production quality is low but the comedy still makes me laugh.

We've had wonderful experiences performing live shows in Montreal, Guelph, and mostly Toronto. I feel we've started to hit our stride, creating some of my all-time favourite scenes: bog monster, milk, Benson family remix, cottage weekend '99, Peebers...

I think our best work lies ahead and I'm excited to create more online content. This time with higher production values.
