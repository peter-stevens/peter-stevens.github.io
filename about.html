ABOUT

Hello world

My name is Peter Stevens. I am a professional writer, comedian, improviser, and teacher living in Toronto.

I was born in Etobicoke, raised in Brampton, worked across Europe and Asia, and can be spotted on the streets of Toronto and Montreal.

MY HISTORY

I went to a high school with an extensive drama program. It was a valuable introduction to performance, directing, playwriting, and improv. I earned History, Politics, and Education degrees from three universities in Ontario.

I continued my passion for comedy by performing for Second City, creating sketch troupes, making videos, and producing shows.

I lead trips for high school students to earn credits while we travel (that's me correcting grammar at 0:10).

My brother, Andrew, helped me build this website.
