My Name is Peter Stevens and these are my projects.

Bet on This Love (2014) is my screenplay about a boyfriend who puts his girlfriend through a break up to win money on a Reality TV game show.

Elephant Empire's "Remember the Dibigong" (2014) is my comedy troupe's TV pilot script. Each episode mixes silly sketches with a twisted, three act play.

Bee Circus (2013) won a Canadian Comedy Award in 2014. I wrote it for CBC's The Irrelevant Show.

The Corrupt One(2012) is an original pilot about an aimless undergrad who rises through university politics to run the whole show.
