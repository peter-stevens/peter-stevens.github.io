Warsport

For millennia, massive galactic wars have melted planets and crushed civilizations. After The Great Oops, bloodshed was declared too costly for life in the universe.

The Cosmic Union replaced war with sport. All interplanetary conflicts are to be settled by organized games. Five Diplomats versus Five Diplomats.

Today, Warsport is the cosmos’ favourite pastime with centillions of fans, pro leagues in each quadrant, planet, and province of the galazy, and arenas in every skycity, ice meadow, needle forest, bog, subterraneon farm, schoolyard -all you need is a field, a ball, and let the war begin!

Every child, egg, and spud in the universe dreams of playing in The Big Game of the Alpha League Championships.

THE SET UP

You could care less about Warsports. You play as Hoffy Vam-a-dan, a fast-talking liar and a loser. You’re a penniless, aging, scam artist running from your creditors, the law, and the alien families that you bankrupted. You’re desperate, destitute, and in serious organ debt to multiple mobs. You’re done dreaming big… it’s time to put yourself out of your misery.

But then a child pulled on your coat, begging for help. So you stepped up and coached her little league friends to a schoolyard victory. You discovered your power. Your fast-talking deception has given you a knack for motivational halftime speeches. Now you’ve had a vision:

YOU as manager of your own Warsport team.

YOUR QUEST

You’re starting below the bottom. Your mission is to hustle a squad together, grind out wins in the black market leagues, and build your roster and reputation.

Raise funds, buy a new identity, acquire a spaceship, get off planet and into the bigger game. There’s colossal coin to be made for mercenaries in political sports.

Seek out the rejects of the galaxy. Assemble a team of hopeless misfits and complete their sidequests to unlock their potential. Upgrade your crew into a cash-winning dynasty of legendary Diplomats. And, who knows, maybe an appearance in the elite Alpha League?

It seems like half the universe is trying to take your money, your team, and your life. But, hey, it’s okay. As long as you have your head you can stay a step ahead. You’re gonna make it. You’re dreaming big again.

Just win, baby. It’s your only shot at redemption.

Game on.

GAMEPLAY

The Sport

Warsport is 5v5, played on field with a ball. It's simplicity gives it intergalactic appeal. There are local variations but the core rules are the same.

A point is scored when a player has possesion of the ball in the endzone.

Generally, players run with the ball, avoid tackles, and throw it to teammates. Play never stops.

The Controls

For every action (tackle, deke, catch, throw) the user can choose to act neutral or press the "finesse" or "brute force" button.

Player Development

New abilities are unlocked through XP and reaching certain narrative milestones in a player's sidequest. The path to "elite player level" is only open once their sidequest is complete. You'll need seven team members to reach "elite player level" to play in the big leagues.

WHAT’S HONEST?

Redemption stories are at the heart of this silly universe. You lie to him nonstop about the holy purpose of the team.

WHAT’S INFORMATIVE?

A rich galaxy of diverse planets and characters. Users can uncover the backstories of any character on their team by entering their sidequest.

WHAT’S ENTERTAINING?

Gameplay, like all sports, is repetitive. We enjoy how practive makes us better, having clear victory goals, and unlocking new powers.
